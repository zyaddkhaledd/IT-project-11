WELCOME TO EGYPT

Egypt is considered one of the oldest civilizations in history, emerging along the banks of the Nile River more than seven thousand years ago. The ancient Egyptians left behind a magnificent legacy of monuments, such as the pyramids, temples, and royal tombs, which continue to amaze the world to this day. These monuments reflect their brilliance in engineering, art, and science.
